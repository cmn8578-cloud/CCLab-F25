Wigglie Woopa

Scientific Name:

Wiggliz

Discovered by / at:

Chau Ngo

Family:

The Wiggliz family

Habitat:

It lives an unknown spaces filled with colorful circles, in places that are far from the Earth.

Appearance:

Wigglie Woopa has a long, flexible body that gets smaller at the tip. It has multiple short legs, side-glancing eyes, and two little ears that pop all the time.

Key Characteristics:

  • Normally, it is always moving and its colors change constantly.
  • When humans interact with it, it gets more excited, so it expands and moves faster.
  • When touch it through a mouse, its colors shift constantly as if twinkling.
  • When interact it through the space bar, it becomes extra plump and chubby.

What we know so far about Wigglisaurus? Wigglie Woopa (from the Wiggliz family) is a worm-like creature that changes colors when they’re happy — not for hiding like chameleons, but to stand out and spread positive energy. They can’t stop moving, as they are always full of excitement.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature 1/ To watch it grow bigger and move faster, slide the mouse from left to right across the canvas. 2/ To see its colors shift and twinkle constantly,click the mouse. 3/ To make it suddenly grow bigger and change its habitat, press any key.
